Three Pillars of Discovery

Integrating cutting-edge techniques to unravel the complexity of neuromodulatory systems

Pillar 01

Large-scale Electrophysiology

Recording from hundreds of neurons simultaneously across prefrontal cortex and basal ganglia using high-density Neuropixels probes.

Neuropixels 2.0
Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)
Basal Ganglia Circuits
Pillar 02

Optical Imaging

Real-time monitoring of neuromodulator dynamics using genetically-encoded sensors and multi-site fiber photometry.

Fiber Photometry
2-Photon Microscopy
GRAB/dLight Sensors

Computational Modeling

Building biologically-informed models of learning and decision-making that operate across multiple timescales.
