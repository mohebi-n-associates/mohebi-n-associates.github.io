        In our lab, we explore how the brain adapts to environmental changes through learning, 
        a key aspect of intelligence. Learning occurs at various timescales, from rapid changes 
        at the level of synapses to gradual adjustments in neurons and brain circuits.
        
        We focus on how psychological states like **arousal**, **stress**, and **motivation** 
        influence these learning processes. Our research centers on understanding how key 
        neuromodulators affect information processing and decision-making.
