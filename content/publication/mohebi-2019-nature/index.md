## Key Findings

1. **Regional dissociation**: Dopamine release patterns differ between ventral and dorsal striatum during motivated behavior

2. **Learning vs motivation**: Mesolimbic dopamine encodes reward prediction errors for learning, while nigrostriatal dopamine signals motivational state

3. **Local regulation**: Motivational dopamine signals appear to be locally regulated in terminal regions, independent of somatic firing

## Significance

This work challenges unified theories of dopamine function and reveals that the neuromodulatory logic of dopamine varies across brain circuits.
